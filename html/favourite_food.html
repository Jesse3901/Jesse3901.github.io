Soup

Recipe by: Jesse Xia

A seafood congee is the rice soup consisting of shrimp, rice, green onion and salt. It is unbelievably delicious and easy to cook.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Shrimp
  • Green onion
  • Salt

Directions:

  1. Cook a regular rice soup
  2. When the rice soup is cooked, add shrimp to the soup
  3. Cook the soup until the shrimp is cooked
  4. Add the proper amount of salt
  5. Add green onion, stir a few times
  6. The delicious seafood congee is ready to serve
